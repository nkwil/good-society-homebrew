A writing room for the cycle.

Email-inspired three-tab phase environment built around the locked v1 composer + letter card. Wraps the writing experience in an inbox, an outbox, and a registry of typed wax seals — yellow casual, red invitation, green burn-after-reading. Custom illustration assets supplied by the GM go in the seal slots.

The burn mechanic

  • Warning eyebrow appears at the top of the detail view in green-positive color. Recipient knows the letter is destructible before they finish reading.
  • "Burn now" action replaces the normal "archive" action when seal is green. Triggers an immediate 1.5s CSS keyframe — the card fades and ages, a small flame rises and disappears.
  • Auto-burn: if the recipient closes the detail view OR navigates away from the wizard for >30s without burning manually, the burn fires anyway. The letter cannot be re-read.
  • After burn: the chat message's content is replaced with "(Burned. The letter is destroyed.)"; the inbox row vanishes for the recipient; the outbox row for the sender shows status "Burned · cycle N." Original content preserved in flags.burnedContent for GM forensics (configurable per-world).
  • Themed render: Avril's candlelight & crimson theme. Letters from her always read on near-black paper with gold accents — burn or no burn.

How it behaves

  • Auto-opens for the GM when cyclePhase becomes epistolary. Auto-closes when the phase advances.
  • Counts are read from chat-message flags scoped to the current cycle. Sent = letters where this actor is the sender; received = letters where this actor is in the recipient list.
  • Status reflects the per-actor flag flags["good-society-homebrew"].epistolaryDone[cycleNumber]. ✓ when set; ⏳ when not.
  • Poke laggers whispers the not-done players a reminder. Unlock all clears the done flag (e.g., GM realized the phase needs more time). Advance phase ↗ calls cycleAdvance() regardless of completion — GM can advance even with stragglers.
  • Row click (not shown) opens a peek of that actor's outbox, read-only.

Custom illustration slots

  • Each seal type has an iconAsset field that points at assets/seals/{seal-id}.png. The placeholder symbols above (✎ ⚜ ★) are CSS-rendered glyphs for the preview only.
  • Asset spec: 64×64 (or larger) transparent PNG / SVG, on-color or two-color flat, designed to read at 24–32 px when rendered in inbox / outbox rows. Should look like a wax seal — circular bias, slight texture, no harsh edges.
  • Future seals (placeholder, not yet defined): black mourning, white ceremonial, blue secret, purple confession. Add to the SEAL_TYPES array in module/constants.js with id, label, color, iconAsset, behavior. The composer's seal picker, the inbox row's disc, and the filter pills all auto-update.
